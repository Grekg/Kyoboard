Frequently Asked Questions

Can I switch plans later?

Yes, you can upgrade or downgrade your plan at any time. Changes to your billing will be prorated automatically.

Do you offer discounts for non-profits?

Absolutely! We love supporting organizations that do good. Contact our sales team for special non-profit pricing.

What happens after my trial ends?

If you don't upgrade by the end of your 14-day trial, your account will be downgraded to the free plan with limited features.
